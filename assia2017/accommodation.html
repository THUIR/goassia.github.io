Hotel Accommodation

Please make a booking of accommodations by yourself (except Hotel Co-op Inn Kyoto described below). Good hotels and hostels can be found in the TripAdvisor website for Kyoto, booking.com or Rakuten Travel.

Hotel Co-op Inn Kyoto (only for students, limited availability)

We have blocked several rooms at Hotel Co-op Inn Kyoto (English page) during the summer school period. It is located in the most central part of Kyoto, and takes 30-35 min. to the venue (Kyoto University) by city bus. The detailed information is as follows:

  • Check-in: April 1st (Tue)
  • Check-out: April 6th (Sun)
  • Room Type: Single room, with breakfast
  • Fee: 36,200 JPY (6,700 JPY for weekdays and 9,400 JPY for weekend)
  • You will be asked to pay the accommodation fee at the hotel eigther in cash or credit card.

If you are a student and prefer to stay at this hotel, please indicate so in the online registration form. We will inform you the status of reservation after confirmation of your application. Note that we only accept those who will check-in April 1st and check-out April 6th.
