Welcome

Welcome to the home page of the 1st Asian Summer School in Information Access (ASSIA 2013). The summer schools in Information Retrieval have been very successful in Europe where research students, young faculty members, or industrial researchers can get a great opportunity to learn the core and advanced topics from the world-class experts in the field. However, no such opportunity was available in most Asian countries. Therefore, we are pleased to announce that the 1st Asian Summer School in Information Access will be held in 2013 at Tsukuba, Japan.

  • Date: August 2-5, 2017
  • Place: Kyoto, Japan
